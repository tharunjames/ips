The best retail POS software Around

iPOS Retail is a leading Point of Sales (POS) software solutions provider in the Middle East, precisely GCC countries. Our retail software iPOS Retail caters to different segments of retail industry that includes;
  • Hypermarkets
  • Supermarkets & Grocery Stores
  • Fashion & Department Stores
  • Cosmetics & Electronics
  • Specialty Stores

The objective of the product is to drive the business as easy as possible with an exciting user friendly interface and its business oriented analysis reports. IPOS Retail is fully loaded with all the tools for a retail business segment covering key operational areas such as Point of Sale, Customer Loyalty program, Inventory Management, Financial Accounting, business intelligence and much more.

Company Hierarchy

Whether you run a single store or a multi-store retail organization, iPOS helps you tailor the system to develop and maintain your company hierarchy & structure. It starts with defining the parent group followed by multiple companies under it, branches or divisions under each company and finally the locations of operations. Hence, the top management always get to have an overview on how the whole organization (consolidated) is performing along with deep insights on the growth and profitability of every cost centre.

payment Bill Payment

Even though the system is loaded with comprehensive financial accounting module , bill payment feature is very handy and very useful tool for the user. This feature allows the user to manage the vendor’s payments against each outstanding invoices after checking the credit period and it’s due date. At the same time, user will be alerted of any deductions that include debit note against GRVs , display rent, listing fee, annual rebates etc.

Data Security & User Access Control

Our solution emphasize on providing high level data security and integrity. While hosting the data server locally, our clients are even offered the flexibility to have an automated data back up on cloud.

POS Fraud Control:

As fraud control is of high priority in retail, iPOS Retail enables you tackle every possible act of fraudulence from every aspect of daily transactions such as sales returns, Cash refunds, bill hold functionality, line voids, discounts, below cost sales etc. Supervisors can be empowered with rights to authorize or turn down any such transaction requests. All entries are logged in our database for any future reference.
