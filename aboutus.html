About Us

iPOS is a reputable software and hardware solutions provider with services targeting different sectors in retail and hospitality market including restaurants, supermarkets, department stores, hypermarkets and many more in the fast expanding retail market across the Middle East.

WHO WE ARE

iPOS first opened its doors in 2007 and has earned an unrivaled reputation as a leader in Point of Sales (POS) software solutions across the Middle East.Our Retail software iPOS Retail is the primary solution in leading supermarkets, hypermarkets, department stores and many more retail outlets across the GCC countries. In addition to the retail Software, iPOS also provide tailored POS systems for restaurants, spa/saloons, barcode solutions, as well as POS hardware.

iPOS started in the United Arab Emirates and later expended to Saudi Arabia and Qatar. Today, our team of profession prides itself with second to none software engineering and retail domain experience.Since our inception, our excellence is fueled by our commitment on excellence and customer centric services. Our persistence pursuit for quality has helped us take our services to new heights and a spot as a leading solution provider for the fast growing retail industry.At iPOS, we pride ourselves on an outstanding track record in successful implementation of retail solutions which is reflects on our long list of clients ready to recommend out services.
