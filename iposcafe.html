iPOS Restaurant Management Software

Our renowned iPos Restaurant management system is effect in providing restaurant owners with the means needed to speed up and boost the efficiency of their business operations. Equipped with customizable features, this system is highly recommended for any type of a restaurant such as fast food chains, bistros, fine dining, and cafes.We pride ourselves with the technology required to cut down the customer wait times, ease and streamline management processes a as well as profit from every revenue opportunity. Our solutions will help you provide customers with smooth service that they are hungry for while helping your business to grow without recruiting more staff.

Our Key Features Important features of iPOS Cafe

Restaurants POS

With our Point of Sale terminals, your business can now provide customers with superior services. Taking orders is now relatively easy and straightforward while providing your customers with up to date information.

Fast order taking

Reward your customers with fast and effective services with our POS terminals. Fine dining restaurants can enjoy pace and mobility thanks to the mobile order making capability. The POS system will send the orders straight to the kitchen system ensuring that your services are not only fast paced but also effective.

Kitchen management and display system

Our Kitchen Management System ensures that you are always in control of the orders and preparations while accessing real time information regarding the status of the orders as the POS terminal to your kitchen processes them. Our system is easy to customize from a central point and rolling out these changes to all other outlets.

As fraud control is of high priority in retail, iPOS Retail enables you tackle every possible act of fraudulence from every aspect of daily transactions such as sales returns, Cash refunds, bill hold functionality, line voids, discounts, below cost sales etc. Supervisors are empowered with rights to authorize or turn down any such transaction requests. Entries are all logged in our database for any future reference.

BARCODE SCANNING

Whether a restaurant includes a retail area, a gift shop, or it packages and sells its specialty items, i-pos cafe can help! i-pos cafe has the ability to set a lookup code for products, and then scan a barcode or key in the lookup code to add the product to an order. Existing UPC barcodes on packages can be used, or a restaurant can print and attach their own bar code to a product.
