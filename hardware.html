Pos Receipt Printers

Compact printer used to print the sales receipt in the POS counter. Two types of printers, Dot Matrix or thermal printers are widely used

Dot matrix Printers: In addition to the paper roll, ink cartridge need to be used. Thermal Printer: Thermal Cash roll is used in this printer. Printing speed Is much faster than the dot matrix type. And the print impact fades faster compared to dot matrix type.

Brands available: Epson, Samsung, Posiflex, Citizen,star


Barcode Printer

Compact printer used to print Barcodes

Thermal transfer or direct thermal technology could be used to pre-design and print barcode and brief description of the item with the selling price on a standard label roll.

Brands available: Zebra, Datamax, Argox, Delsol
